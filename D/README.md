# Blockchain-Terminologies - D

<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-bMXXhtYOWX8/X-2yCeuhAxI/AAAAAAAAAzs/N2XJ_IfYVVkN5krt-P9KvrTS6Xn6t82KwCLcBGAsYHQ/s595/BCT-D.jpeg" width="170" align="center">
</p>  

- **Double Spending** - Double Spending is a potential flaw in digital cash transaction system where the same digital money can be spent more than once.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NUogAGulxMg/X-2yCepX93I/AAAAAAAAAzw/ge7Hv6BVK-gRKlAqryzCuKhFvs6NWgBsQCLcBGAsYHQ/s791/BCT-D1.jpeg" width="370" align="center">
</p>  

- **Decentralization** - Decentralization is the process of dispersing the resources, functions or powers from a central location or authority, where there is no single point of failure.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fU7i_Dic0gU/X-2yDDNyknI/AAAAAAAAAz4/7VIAc6Gqa64erK9nu4qH7hrXbX5hlYaqQCLcBGAsYHQ/s867/BCT-D2.jpeg" width="250" align="center">
</p>  

- **Distributed** - Distributed is the process of distributing the resources and functions to all systems located on different networked computers, which communicate and coordinate their actions by passing messages to one another.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3d-RrThcl1w/X-2yDWP5SiI/AAAAAAAAA0A/8qJRREnIduANZOr-YZpvJLyEhr6NKoz8QCLcBGAsYHQ/s723/BCT-D3.jpeg" width="230" align="center">
</p>  

- **Digital Signature** - Digital Signature is a cryptographic primitive to verify the authenticity and integrity of the message where signing refers to encrypting hash of the message using the private key.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-YDcnM_0vU2g/X-2yDasfz0I/AAAAAAAAAz8/gcWJ3VOySc8y2w79w0M2jID66jyWLV8IwCLcBGAsYHQ/s553/BCT-D4.jpeg" width="210" align="center">
</p>  

- **DLT** - Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) is a peer-to-peer consensus driven network of shared and distributed ledger. Blockchain Technology is one form of DLT.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-DxZPZKlQhHI/X-2yEHLK3LI/AAAAAAAAA0E/03N8iAKH9fgRElBoQc3Vmm7th5aRmPq1ACLcBGAsYHQ/s916/BCT-D5.jpeg" width="380" align="center">
</p>  

- **DAG** - Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) is a directed graph with no directed cycles which is used to model many different kinds of information.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-C38WSDc8Hs8/X-2yEImKNnI/AAAAAAAAA0I/M8nTOQ966m4mUcsWwq1_B4VCpBKfUkIQQCLcBGAsYHQ/s473/BCT-D6.jpeg" width="220" align="center">
</p>  

- **Difficulty** - Difficulty is the value or measure to determine how hard it is to mine a block or to find a hash value below the given target.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eP4rnkfdYUk/X-2yELdbT2I/AAAAAAAAA0M/a0ofF-nOpS8t-hkvPJtDhFutiFyrqH4LgCLcBGAsYHQ/s545/BCT-D7.jpeg" width="300" align="center">
</p>  

- **DApp** - Distributed Application (DApp) is an application that runs on a Distributed Ledger Technology like Ethereum Blockchain. DApp is a collection of smart contracts and User Interface.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ur5Uzn67ytE/X-2yEwjK0bI/AAAAAAAAA0Q/Q9--CNqEHt8P0jnlgQEYy4F5JQ3wOZsngCLcBGAsYHQ/s817/BCT-D8.jpeg" width="330" align="center">
</p>  

- **DAO** - Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) is an organization represented by logic & rules as a digital program typically in form of smart contracts that is controlled by the organization members where the transactions and the contracts are maintained on a blockchain.
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-d4nkeY1ba68/X-2yFFjiHrI/AAAAAAAAA0U/mtwAIHTxqLEDZ9H00VVQKtEp-aQyLtFkwCLcBGAsYHQ/s657/BCT-D9.jpeg" width="300" align="center">
</p>  

- **DeFi** - Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is the ecosystem of peer-to-peer cryptocurrency based financial DApp. 
<p align="center">
<img src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kbJ1LHvfu1k/X-2yCfpx6DI/AAAAAAAAAz0/m0sTUQaYqIsB_clzlxQD34WR3pZaRsdHACLcBGAsYHQ/s807/BCT-D10.jpeg" width="330" align="center">
</p>
